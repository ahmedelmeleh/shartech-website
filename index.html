About Shartech

A Saudi company built to deliver infrastructure solutions with the depth and accountability the market demands.

Shartech is headquartered in Riyadh and operates across three specialized divisions: Shartech ICT for telecom power infrastructure, Shartech Power for energy distribution and renewables, and Shartech Smart for AI and intelligent technology systems.

Our strength lies in combining a high-calibre local team with direct access to certified, globally proven products giving clients the technical confidence of an international supplier with the responsiveness and accountability of a local partner.

We serve Saudi Arabia's telecom operators, energy companies, giga projects, and government clients wherever reliable, certified infrastructure is required.
